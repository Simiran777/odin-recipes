Grilled Chicken Breast

Description

Whether you keep them on hand for quick lunches or serve them for dinner tonight, a juicy Grilled Chicken Breast is the perfect way to upgrade any meal. Lean, healthy, and versatile, grilled chicken breasts are full of potential (paired up with Pasta for starters and Chocolate Banana Bread, YOU CAN SURELY THROW A PARTY!).

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • Chicken Marinade (No matter which marinade you choose, it is critical to create the juiciest and most flavorful chicken breast.)
  • Any of your favourite toppings (p.s. I love this Avocado Crunch!!!)

Steps

  1. Pound the chicken breasts.
  2. Poke holes in the chicken.
  3. Add the chicken to a ziptop bag with the marinade. Seal and let marinate at room temperature (for at least 30 minutes) or in the refrigerator (for up to 12 hours).
  4. Let the chicken come to room temperature (if refrigerated). Grill chicken directly on the grill grates at 475 degrees F for 4 to 5 minutes on the first side and 4 to 8 minutes on the other side
  5. Let it rest for a few minutes, add toppings, and DIG IN!
