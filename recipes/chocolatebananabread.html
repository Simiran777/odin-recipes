Chocolate Banana Bread

Description

Healthy Double Chocolate Banana Bread is tender, moist, and tastes like a fudgy brownie! This recipe is made with almond flour and perfectly sweetened with ripe bananas and a touch of pure maple syrup. It is paleo, dairy free and gluten free and can also be made vegan. The perfect way to use up your overripe bananas!

Ingredients

  • Almond flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking essentials
  • Bananas
  • Eggs
  • Maple syrup
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and line a loaf pan with parchment paper
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the almond flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  3. Place the bananas in a medium bowl. Use a potato masher or fork to mash the bananas until they are almost smooth.
  4. Add the eggs, maple syrup and vanilla to the bananas and stir to combine.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in half of the chocolate chips.
  6. Carefully pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips evenly over the top of the batter. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the bread is fragrant and set through the center.
  7. Cool completely. Remove from the loaf pan, slice and enjoy!
