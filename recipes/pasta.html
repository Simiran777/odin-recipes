Pasta

Description

This Easy One-Pot Pasta is the perfect weeknight dinner - uncooked pasta, veggies, and sauce combine and cook together in one pot in only 20 minutes! One-Pot Pasta. Also known as the ultimate cozy weeknight dinner because it is quick, easy, satisfying, and easy to pack with veggies.

Ingredients

  • Dry Pasta (Uncooked)
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Water
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Veggies!

Steps

  1. Pour the pasta on the bottom of the pot or pan
  2. Add the remaining ingredients
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then simmer until the pasta is al-dente
  4. Serve and enjoy!
